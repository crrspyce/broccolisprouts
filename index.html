About

Carrie Cantrell

Founder, Broccoli Sprouts Local Economy Platform. Based in Brevard, North Carolina.

I am a digital communications professional, journalist, and community organizer with over a decade of experience building media projects, training production teams, and supporting people in telling their own stories. My work sits at the intersection of technology, storytelling, and economic empowerment.

I believe the best businesses grow like the best food: with patience, community, and good roots. Broccoli Sprouts exists to help people in the Appalachian region build sustainable livelihoods through practical coaching, digital infrastructure, and honest strategy.
